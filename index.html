About SRMJEEE

The SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination (SRMJEEE) is the common entrance gateway for admission to B.Tech programs across all SRM campuses. It is a computer-based test designed to assess candidates' proficiency in core science subjects.

Phase 1 Important Dates

Event Tentative Date
Application Deadline April 15, 2026
Phase 1 Exam Date April 20 - 22, 2026

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nationality: Resident Indians, Non-Resident Indians (NRIs), and PIO or OCI cardholders.
  • Academic: Passed or appearing in 10+2 (Class 12) examination.
  • Minimum Marks: 60% aggregate in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology.
